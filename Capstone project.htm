As a background information, Istanbul is the largest city in Turkey with a population of 15 million people. Kadikoy is one of the 39 districts of Istanbul. Note: For the people who are not familiar with Istanbul or Kadikoy, I think it may be good to know this piece of additional information: Kadikoy may refer to (a) the district of Kadikoy as a whole (b) the area within this district, where Kadikoy Piers, Kadikoy Bus Stops and Kadikoy Metro Station or even the Kadikoy Bazaar are located. So the term Kadikoy changes on the context it is used. For instance, if one is talking about the election results in Kadikoy, he is referring to meaning (a), and if two friends are talking about meeting at Kadikoy, they are probably referring to meaning (b).
